Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need my exact birth time for a Vedic reading?+
An exact birth time is ideal for full chart accuracy, particularly for the Rising sign and house placements. If you don't have it, we can work with an approximate time or use a Janma Kundali technique to narrow it down. Please share whatever you have — a reading is always possible.
What is the difference between Vedic and Western astrology?+
Vedic (Jyotish) astrology uses the sidereal zodiac — the actual positions of stars in the sky — while Western astrology uses the tropical zodiac, which is fixed to the seasons. Vedic astrology also places far greater emphasis on the Moon, the rising sign (Lagna), and the Dasha system for prediction. Many people find their Vedic chart reflects their inner life more precisely.
Can I get a tarot reading without a Vedic chart?+
Absolutely. Tarot stands completely on its own and requires no birth data. You bring an intention or question, and the cards create the space for insight. The Astro-Tarot Synthesis reading is the only offering that combines both — all other tarot sessions are fully self-contained.
Are sessions available online?+
Yes — all readings are available via video call, and recordings are shared afterward. In-person sessions in Kolkata can be arranged on request. The quality and depth of the reading are identical regardless of format.
How often should I get a reading?+
For ongoing guidance, one reading every 6–12 months is a natural rhythm — aligned with major Dasha shifts or significant life changes. A Dasha & Transit reading is particularly valuable at the start of each year. That said, there are no rules — follow what your soul asks for.
What languages are readings offered in?+
Readings are available in English and Bengali. Please specify your preference at the time of booking.
